## Bulk Email Setup Tips

### Tools
- **Mailchimp:** Free for <500 contacts, good for sequences
- **HubSpot:** More powerful, better tracking, free tier available
- **Lemlist:** Better for personalized cold email (auto-insert company name, etc.)
- **Outreach:** Enterprise-grade, expensive, best for large teams

### Process
1. **Build Contact List:** CSV of email addresses (first name, company, title optional)
2. **Personalize:** Use merge tags {{FirstName}}, {{CompanyName}} for personalization
3. **Test:** Send to yourself first, check formatting
4. **Schedule:** Stagger sends (don't send all 100 at once = spam filter)
5. **Track:** Monitor open rates, click rates, replies
6. **Follow-Up:** Email #2 → Email #3 based on engagement

### Expected Results (Per 100 Emails)
- **Open Rate:** 35-45% (depending on subject line)
- **Click Rate:** 5-10% (click link to ProformAI landing page)
- **Reply Rate:** 5-10% (reply to email)
- **Meeting Rate:** 1-3% (agree to discovery call)
- **Pilot Rate:** 0.5-1% (sign up for pilot)
- **Customer Rate:** 0.2-0.5% (convert to paid customer)

**To get 5 pilots:** Send ~500-1000 emails  
**To get 2 customers:** Expect ~1000-2000 emails over 4-6 weeks

### Cost Analysis
- **Time:** 20-30 hours to build list + sequences
- **Tools:** $0-500/month (Mailchimp free, HubSpot $45-3200/month)
- **Revenue Impact:** 2-5 customers × $1,200-2000/month = $2,400-10,000 MRR
- **Payback:** 2-6 weeks (assuming 50% close rate on pilots)

## Tracking & Optimization

### Metrics to Monitor
1. **Open Rate:** Indicates subject line effectiveness
2. **Click Rate:** Indicates copy effectiveness
3. **Reply Rate:** Indicates relevance + interest
4. **Meeting Rate:** Indicates qualification
5. **Pilot Rate:** Indicates value proposition clarity
6. **Close Rate:** Indicates product quality

### Weekly Optimization
- **Poor open rate (<25%):** Revise subject line
- **Good open, poor click (<3%):** Revise email copy (make CTA clearer)
- **Good click, poor reply (<5%):** Add calendar link (reduce friction to meeting)
- **Good reply, no meeting:** Your discovery call ask was unclear (revise)
